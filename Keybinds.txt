++CAMERA CONTROLS

'o' or '0' = Reset Camera Position

TRANSLATION:
--- 'WASD' ========= X,Z Locals
--- Space/LShift === Up/Down
--- 'R'/'F' ======== ZoomIN/ZoomOut (FoV)
--- '-' ============ -FarPlane
--- '=' ============ +FarPlane
--- '[' ============ -NearPlane
--- ']' ============ +NearPlane

ROTATION:
--- UP arrow ======= Look up
--- Down arrow ===== Look down
--- Left arrow ===== Look Left
--- Right arrow ==== Look Right
